## Idea 1: Bold African Modernism

**Design Movement:** Afrofuturism meets Swiss Modernism — structured, confident, forward-looking.

**Core Principles:**
- Strong geometric shapes inspired by East African textile patterns
- High contrast between earthy terracotta/ochre and deep forest green
- Editorial-style typography with dramatic scale contrasts
- Asymmetric layouts that feel intentional and dynamic

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Deep Forest Green (#1A3D2B) — growth, nature, Nandi highlands
- Accent: Warm Ochre/Gold (#D4A017) — energy, youth, optimism
- Neutral: Off-white (#F5F0E8) — warmth, approachability
- Dark: Charcoal (#1C1C1E) — authority, seriousness

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed hero with diagonal text placement
- Alternating left/right content blocks with large section numbers
- Sticky side-navigation for long pages
- Cards with strong left-border accents

**Signature Elements:**
- Bold section numbering (01, 02, 03) in large faded type
- Geometric diamond/triangle motifs from Nandi cultural patterns
- Horizontal rule dividers with color accent

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Scroll-triggered fade-in for content blocks
- Hover: cards lift with subtle shadow + color shift
- Navigation: underline grows from left on hover

**Animation:**
- Entrance: staggered slide-up (60ms delay per item, 400ms ease-out)
- Hero text: letter-spacing expansion on load
- Section transitions: fade + slight translateY

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display Bold — authoritative, editorial
- Body: DM Sans Regular — clean, modern, accessible
- Accent: DM Sans Semibold for labels and captions

## Idea 2: Civic Energy — Bold & Grassroots

**Design Movement:** Contemporary civic design meets street-level energy — raw, honest, impactful.

**Core Principles:**
- High-energy color blocking inspired by protest graphics and civic campaigns
- Oversized typography as a design element, not just content
- Grid-breaking layouts that feel alive and urgent
- Photography-forward with strong overlay treatments

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Deep Teal (#0D5C63) — trust, stability, water (Nandi rivers)
- Accent: Vibrant Amber (#F5A623) — action, urgency, youth energy
- Background: Warm White (#FAFAF7)
- Dark: Near-black (#111827)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Oversized hero with full-bleed background and bold headline
- Masonry-style activity grid
- Horizontal scrolling section for programs/committees
- Statistics displayed as large typographic statements

**Signature Elements:**
- Color-blocked quote sections with full-width background
- Bold stat counters with animated number roll
- Diagonal section dividers

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Magnetic hover on CTA buttons
- Parallax scrolling on hero image
- Smooth anchor scrolling with progress indicator

**Animation:**
- Hero: text slides in from bottom, 500ms ease-out
- Stats: count-up animation on scroll into view
- Cards: scale 1.02 on hover with shadow

**Typography System:**
- Display: Space Grotesk Bold — modern, techy, youth-forward

## CHOSEN DESIGN: Idea 2 — Civic Energy (Bold & Grassroots)

**Rationale:** NYA is a youth-driven civic organization. The design needs to feel energetic, credible, and inspiring — not corporate or bureaucratic. Bold typography, high-contrast color blocking, and photography-forward layouts communicate the urgency and passion of the assembly's mission.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep Teal `oklch(0.38 0.08 195)` 
- Accent: Amber `oklch(0.75 0.16 75)`
- Background: Warm White `oklch(0.99 0.003 95)`
- Dark: Near-black `oklch(0.12 0.005 285)`

**Fonts:** Space Grotesk (display/headings) + Nunito (body)
